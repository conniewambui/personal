Who am I?

I am a girl,
A young mother,
I have been judged,
Critised,
Ridiculed,
I judge my existence,
Who am I?
My wings clipped,
My dreams are not valid,
Walking in the shadows,
Who am I?,
I am someone's daughter,
mother,
sister,
Freedom I crave,
Who will free me?,
I will change my story,
I see my dawn,
Poverty is not my portion,
I will rise amidst the crumbles,
I will rise!
